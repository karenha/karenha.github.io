Here's my story....

As I was studying biology, I had to take a course where I had to code to complete a lab experiment. It was incredibly intimidating for my class and I because nobody had any coding experience. Everyone struggled. I remember dropping everything I was doing to figure out how to code. It was a frutrating process, but very satisfying to see what code can do. At that moment, I fell in love with coding. This is a passion that I knew I had to explore further, so I decided to do just that. Since then, I became obsessed with the recent web development trends. Whenever I visit a new website, I always stop to look at how everything is working. I knew I had to pursue web development as a career.

I shifted my interest from biology to web development. When I started taking courses, I remember being the only female in one of my classes. In my first group project, I was also the only female in a group of six. It turns out women only make up about 25% of computing roles. Despite the pattern, I am more determined than ever to make a difference in the technology industry. Together, we can work on bridging the gender gap. I hope I can be the success story to empower more women to join the technology industry.

"When women support each other, amazing things happen."
